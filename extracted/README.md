## 🎯 **5 NEW MODULES (Professional + Revenue-Generating)**

### 1. 🎙️ **PRONUNCIATION GUIDE** 
**Why it's important:** Most English learners mispronounce professional words. This module fixes that.

**Features:**
- 12 essential business words with phonetic breakdown
- Color-coded by difficulty (Easy 🟢 | Medium 🟡 | Hard 🔴)
- Play word → syllables → normal speed progression
- Interactive filters to learn by level
- Example sentences for each word
- Works with browser's built-in speech synthesis

**Words Included:**
- "strength" (common mispronunciation)
- "entrepreneur"  
- "collaborate"
- "initiative"
- "articulate"
- etc.

**Sellable to:** Language schools, ESL centers, corporate training departments

---

### 2. 🎧 **LISTENING COMPREHENSION EXERCISES**
**Why it's important:** Interviews are LISTENING, not just reading. This trains the ear.

**Features:**
- 3 professional scripts (Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced)
- Actual interview answers read aloud
- Text-to-speech in natural English
- Comprehension questions after each
- Instant scoring and feedback
- Transcript toggle (practice without reading first)

**Scripts Included:**
1. "Tell me about yourself" (45 seconds)
2. "Discussing a weakness" (40 seconds) 
3. "STAR method story" (55 seconds)

**Sellable to:** Universities, online schools, corporations preparing for interviews

---

### 3. 💡 **BUSINESS IDIOMS DICTIONARY**
**Why it's important:** Native speakers use idioms constantly. Learners don't understand them.

**Features:**
- 12 professional idioms with meanings
- Level tags (Common ⭐ | Intermediate 🔷)
- Usage context for each
- Example sentences
- Filterable by difficulty

**Idioms Covered:**
- "Hit the ground running"
- "Think outside the box"
- "Low-hanging fruit"
- "Synergy"
- "Drop the ball"
- "Bandwidth"
- etc.

**Sellable to:** Corporate English programs, professional coaching services

### 4. ⏱️ **SPEAKING TIMER / CHALLENGES**
**Why it's important:** Interviews have time limits. Speaking speed matters for fluency.

**Features:**
- 3 timed challenges (30 sec, 2 min, 1 min)
- Visual timer with progress ring (changes color: 🟢 → 🟡 → 🔴)
- Audio alerts when time is up
- Sample answers for reference
- Speaking tips for each challenge
- Stress-free way to practice pacing

**Challenges:**
1. **30-Second Elevator Pitch** - Introduce yourself professionally
2. **2-Minute STAR Story** - Tell a behavioral story with structure
3. **60-Second Weakness** - Explain growth area professionally

**Sellable to:** Interview coaching centers, executive communication trainers

**Features:**
- 4 professional templates
- Click to copy, customize, and use
- [Bracketed] placeholders for easy personalization
- One-click copy to clipboard
- Includes detailed examples

**Templates:**
1. **Cover Letter** - Professional job application letter
